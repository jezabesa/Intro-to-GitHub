Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Clarrel's Skincare Central collects, uses, and protects personal information when using our appointment system.

Information We Collect

a. Personal Information: Users may provide personal details such as name, contact information, and relevant medical history for appointment scheduling.
b. Usage Information: We collect data on user interactions with our appointment system, including log information and IP addresses.

Use of Information

a. Personal information is used for appointment scheduling, management, and communication.
b. Aggregated, anonymized data may be utilized for statistical analysis and system improvement.

Data Security

a. We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard personal information.
b. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Third-Party Services

a. Third-party services may be employed to facilitate appointment scheduling, and necessary information may be shared with them.
b. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of integrated third-party services.

Cookies

a. Cookies may be used to enhance user experience and gather information on usage patterns.
b. Users can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings.

Data Retention

a. Personal information is retained for the duration necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy.
b. Users can request the deletion of their data after completing their appointments..

User Rights

a. Users have the right to access, correct, or delete their personal information.
b. Requests related to user rights can be submitted through the contact information provided in this policy.

Updates to Privacy Policy

a. We reserve the right to update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices.
b. Users will be notified of significant changes, and the latest version will be available on our website.

Children's Privacy

a. Our appointment system is not intended for individuals under the age of 18.
b. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.
